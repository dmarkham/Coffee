# Trader Joe's Sulawesi

### Description
Notes: Caramel, Earthy, Spice (typical Sulawesi character: full body, low-to-moderate acidity)

ROAST: Medium
ORIGIN: Indonesia, Sulawesi
PROCESS: Typically wet-hulled (Giling Basah) — characteristic of Indonesian coffees

A limited / rotating small-lot offering from Trader Joe's. Sulawesi beans are known for sweetness, body, and spice notes; TJ's bag highlights caramel notes that mellow as the cup cools. Earthy undertone is common to the region.
